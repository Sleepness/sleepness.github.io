Philosophy

Write health code!

If you write reliable, flexible and well documented code, you don't need to take care of your security when you code will be maintained by psychopathic-maniac who know where do you live.

Test your code!

If your code is well tested, it guarantee high quality and that you are can be sure, that your application don't have "strange" behavior. And as well, Symfony2 cats will be happy!

Do not sleep!
